COFW is a chapter of Romance Writers of America™. Those seeking membership in COFW must be members of RWA™. Not a member? No problem! Click the Join RWA™ button below to register. After registering with RWA™, you can join COFW using the COFW Membership button below. If you're already a member of RWA™ and want to renew your COFW membership or join COFW, use the 2017 COFW Membership button below.

COFW Membership is $25 per year. There is a $5 late fee for renewing members who pay after January 31. For questions regarding membership to COFW, please contact cofwtreasurer@gmail.com. Also, dues are not deductible as a charitable contribution but may qualify as a business expense.
If you have any questions about COFW before joining, please feel free to contact us.
