2019 Annual Ignite the Flame Contest

COFW offers our congratulations to these finalists, listed in alphabetical order:

  • Vicki Briner for a Romantic Suspense: Fame Fatale
  • Yanicke Forfang for a Young Adult: A Whisper of Death
  • Renee Wildes for a Paranormal: Seditious Hearts

Our three finalists have been submitted to our final-round judge, New York Times best-selling romance author Karen Harper. All will be refunded their entry fee. The Grand-prize winner will receive a copy edit of their full manuscript, up to 150,000 words, by Enterprise Book Services—a $300 value—that will prepare the manuscript for submission or self-publication. COFW will announce the grand prize winner on Monday, July 15, 2019. Thank you, contestants! Our judges fell in love with our 2019 entries. Judges ranked more than one fourth of the manuscripts as being on the cusp of publishable, with an average score of 95% or more of the available points. Around July 15, 2019, all writers will receive their first-round score sheets, plus copies of their manuscript if the judge supplied notes in-text. Thank you, again, to all the talented writers who participated in our contest, and to the 32 judges who volunteered their time and expertise.
