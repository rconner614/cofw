About COFW

Since 1987, our mission is to promote excellence in romantic fiction, helping writers to become published and establish careers in their writing field.

The Central Ohio Fiction writers believe in the inherent right of all romance writers' voices to be heard. We stand behind diversity, equity, and inclusion as the fabric weaving our chapter together. We commit to taking actionable steps to increase our chapter's diversity. We understand that the greater the diversity, the greater the opportunities for all of us to form an unequalled writing community.
