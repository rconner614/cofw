COFW is a chapter of Romance Writers of America™. Those seeking membership in COFW must be members of RWA™. Not a member? No problem! Click the Join RWA™ button below to register. After registering with RWA™, you can join COFW using the COFW Membership button below.

COFW membership year is Jan-Dec, regardless of when you join. However, if you join in November or December, your membership will also be good for the following year. Price is $30 for new members. Current members will receive a $5 discount if they renew before 1/31. After that date they will be considered a new member.

For questions regarding membership to COFW, please contact cofwtreasurer@gmail.com. Also, dues are not deductible as a charitable contribution but may qualify as a business expense.

If you have any questions about COFW before joining, please feel free to contact us.
