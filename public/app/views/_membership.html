Become a COFW Member!

Thank you for your interest!

CCOFW membership year is Jan-Dec, regardless of when you join. However, if you join in November or December, your membership will also be good for the following year. Price is $50 per year, or $25 if you are a student. Members must renew before 1/31 each year to retain access to the members-only Facebook group and email list-serve. All members must be at least 18 years old.

For the remainder of 2021, we are not charging a fee.

For questions regarding membership to COFW, please contact cofwtreasurer@gmail.com. Also, dues are not deductible as a charitable contribution but may qualify as a business expense.

If you have any questions about COFW before joining, please feel free to contact us.
